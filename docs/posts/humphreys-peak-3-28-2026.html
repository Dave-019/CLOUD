Humphreys Peak - 3/28/2026

On Saturday I hiked Humphreys Peak for the 3rd time this year, this time taking a friend with me. We started the hike at 7 AM (lol I woke up at 3:30), and reached the summit by 11:20 AM. We hiked pretty fast considering how much snow, ice, and bonkers wind there was. I measured a maximum wind speed of 46 mph 😳, and 38°F temperature at the top, next time we'll bring warmer gloves. Afterwards we stopped at Mama B's for much deserved burgers and fries. The burgers were really good, but I still prefer Diablo Burger. 🤤
